List of cool facts about cats;

  1. Unique nose print: A cat's nose has a unique pattern of ridges, similar to a human fingerprint.
  2. Super senses: They can jump up to five times their own height, their low-light vision is six times better than humans, and they have 32 muscles in each ear to allow them to swivel 180 degrees.
  3. Specialized for movement: A cat's collarbone is not attached to other bones, which allows them to squeeze through tight spaces.
